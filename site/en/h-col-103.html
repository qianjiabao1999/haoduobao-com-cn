ABOUT US
Haoduobao 2-yuan store chain is a leader in the "2-yuan leisure lifestyle brand" and a well-known brand in the Chinese small commodity industry. After more than 20 years of development, with a strong sense of mission towards the development of a new model of Chinese department store retail, we have been dedicated to expanding our territory on the retail front. In the future, Duobao will fully utilize the online and offline dual store "new service" model, committed to creating a first-class department store life experience service provider. We adhere to the brand concept of "life is beautiful" and are determined to promote high-quality and low-priced products made in China to the world. Many treasures adhere to the principle of "living The corporate philosophy of "Live Better, Low Price, and Quality" and the positioning of "2 yuan for the entire venue", with the fast fashion department store chain model as the brand core, adopt the fast fashion business model and strong supply chain management of over 30000 products, and win the pursuit of global consumers. Currently, the global stores exceed 10000, covering cities around the world such as Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Beijing, and Shanghai.
